🏢 HOD (Head of Department) Side – ProjectHub

The HOD acts as the department supervisor. Unlike the Admin, who manages the whole platform, the HOD focuses only on projects within their department.

For example:

CSE HOD sees only CSE projects.
AI & DS HOD sees only AI & DS projects.
